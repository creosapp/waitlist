FAQ

Common questions.

Is creos actually available now, or is this a waitlist for something coming soon?
creos is live and working. Early access members get full access to the platform from day one — Media Kit Page, deal pipeline, brand CRM, revenue dashboard, and analytics. It's invite-only while we onboard our first cohort carefully.
What's the difference between Starter and Pro?
Starter gives you the full core platform — Media Kit Page, pipeline, CRM, and revenue tracking — at $19/month. Pro adds advanced analytics with MoM tracking, stale deal alerts, invite codes, and priority access to every new feature as it ships, at $29/month.
What does the free tier include?
The free tier gives you a live Media Kit Page at yourname.creosapp.com — including your profile, social stats, portfolio, past collaborations, and a Send Enquiry button so brands can contact you directly. The pipeline, CRM, and revenue tools require a paid plan.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — every paid plan comes with a 30-day free trial. Your card is required upfront but you won't be charged until day 31. Cancel anytime before then and you'll never pay a thing.
Who is creos built for?
Creators, photographers, videographers, and anyone who gets paid to create. If you're managing brand deals, client work, or partnerships — and doing it through a combination of DMs, PDFs, and spreadsheets — creos was built for you.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is yours. You can export your deals, contacts, and revenue to CSV at any time from your settings — before or after cancelling. We don't hold your data hostage and we never will.
